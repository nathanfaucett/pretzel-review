Brewhouse

6.2/10

1/11/2025

This is a classically shaped American style pretzel. The deep fry gives it a lovely flavor and texture. Do to its size it is more dense and chewy than the larger classic bavarian style but overall is a worth while endeavor. We did not partake in the add on queso but we not disappointed by their delicious house made mustard, a unique flavor experience and a must try.

Flavor: 7.5/10

Sides: 5/10
